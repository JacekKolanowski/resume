Jacek L. Kolanowski

I am a recognised leader, educator and expert in interdisciplinary research across the fields of molecular probes, biosensing, biophysical chemistry, imaging and chemical biology. My strong experience in managing and conducting highly innovative research has earned me the position of the youngest group leader in history at my current Institute and allowed me to attract significant national funding. I want to further my contributions in unveiling the molecular mysteries of disease by leading my own research group and participating in scientific outreach and policy-making.
